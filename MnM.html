Monsters & Mercinaries

Monsters & Mercinaries is a Inscryption inspired Deckbuilder. Its a 2v2 duel game with an heavy focus on cost mechanics. The goal of the game is to have the last card standing on the board.

The game has been in playtesting for the past year with a little over 100 basic playtesting cards. I plan to translate these cards into digital using photshop for the art. Hopefully to get an eventually digital release

Gameplay

Each player builds a deck of 8 cards from the card pool. They shuffle the deck and draw three. There are five slots for each player to play their cards into. Each card has attack and health if a card is placed in the opposing space of an enemy card they will battle. Both cards will subtract the other card attack from their total health and if the cards health decreases to 0 or lower the card is discarded and any coins on the card go to the enemy player. If a player choose not to play any cards for more than one turn 2 of their edge slots will be removed. Any cards there will be moved to the next closest slot or discarded if there are no slots to move to on your side of the board. The game ends when a player can't play any more cards, that player loses and the other player wins.

Rules Pages

The game uses a sigil based inscryption system. Inspired by the game Inscryption. This means that cards abilities are refrenced using sigils marked on the cards. If you look throught the rule book you can see the meaning of all of these sigils. This method allows for simple use of space on the card. It encorages memorization of cards and their abilities.
