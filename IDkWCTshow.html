The IDkWCT Show

The IDkWCT show is an online animated webshow that I have been working on for the past three years. I animate, draw, voice act, and write for every aspect of the show.

The show itself is based are the Object Show format. I want to use this format to critque the Object Show community and the artistic process in general. If your aware I plan to make into shows like ONE or OBS.

The show has currently been on a sort of hiatus for the past 3 years. In this time I've been workinmg on the reboot of the series which is planned to have a held back release to have all the episodes released in a relatively small timespan.
